About the project

AGL approached to develop a website

AGL approached us to develop a website that accurately represents their brand and showcases the design solutions they offer. Our team at Pixelflames thoroughly analyzed the requirements and delivered a unique and high-performing website solution with clear call to actions.

This website highlights their unique kitchen and interior solutions. Pixelflames team carefully handcrafted a top performing website which clearly highlights the quality of clients interior design services and solutions. A user friendly content management system (CMS) is developed for the clients to manage all content of the website without any trouble.The client is very happy with-it the mobile friendly website and CMS solution provided and it is successfully generating sales.

Technology used

Wordpress, HTML5, CSS3, Ajax, Jquery
